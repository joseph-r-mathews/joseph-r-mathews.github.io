Sequential Monte Carlo for Cut-Bayesian Posterior Computation

Description: Internship at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) from 05/23-08/23 focusing on developing an algorithm for efficient sampling of cut-Bayes posterior distributions using sequential Monte Carlo methods. Applications included parameter inference for a modular Bayesian model of ethylene oxide production where the "feedback" from a subset of parameters is "cut" due to model misspecification.
